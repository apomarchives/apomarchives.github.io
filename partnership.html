The Problem

Every missed preservation window is a lost case.

The reactive model — calling in a digital forensics expert after the damage is done — is the most expensive way to handle digital evidence. Platform data ages out in days. Fabricated evidence goes unchallenged. Preservation windows close before anyone thinks to act.

The embedded model solves this before it starts.

"Every unchallenged fabricated screenshot is a liability. Every call that starts with 'we already submitted the evidence' is a conversation that should have happened at intake."

Is This Right for Your Firm?

The Strategic Legal Partnership is designed for attorneys who:

  • Handle family law, civil stalking, or harassment litigation regularly
  • Are tired of digital evidence getting challenged or excluded at trial
  • Want fabricated opposing evidence identified before it misleads the court
  • Need preservation letters out at intake — not three weeks into the case
  • Want a forensics expert integrated into the team, not parachuted in at the last minute
  • Handle enough active matters that a per-case model is inefficient
