Legal — CourtReady

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2026 | Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Short version: CourtReady runs file screening locally in your browser. The content of the files you screen is not transmitted to or stored on APOM Research's servers. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties except as required by law. This page explains, in plain terms, exactly what we collect, what we don't, and why.

Who We Are

CourtReady is a subscription web application published by APOM Research, built for licensed attorneys and their authorized staff. This policy is separate from, and does not apply to, our StayGone application or our APOM Investigations professional services, each of which has its own privacy policy or engagement terms.

Contact: hello@apomarchives.com

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate your account, process billing, and provide support:

  • Account information — your name, email address, firm or organization name, and any bar or licensing information you choose to provide when you register.
  • Billing information — processed by our third-party payment processor. We receive limited transaction and subscription-status data back from the processor; we do not directly store full payment card numbers on our own servers.
  • Support communications — anything you send us directly, such as emails requesting help.
  • Basic technical and log data — such as login timestamps, IP address at login, and browser/device type, used for account security, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting.

What We Do Not Collect: Your File Content

CourtReady's file screening runs locally in your web browser. When you upload a file into CourtReady for screening, the analysis of that file's content is performed on your own device, inside your browser session.

The underlying file content, and the substantive contents of any document you screen, are not transmitted to APOM Research's servers and are not stored by us, unless you take an affirmative, separate action to export, download, or explicitly submit a copy to us (for example, attaching a file to a support email). Screening reports and generated document templates likewise remain in your local browser session or device unless you choose to save, download, or export them yourself.

Because file analysis happens on your device, we do not have visibility into, and cannot access, the specific documents, evidence, or client materials you screen using CourtReady in the ordinary course of using the app.

You remain responsible for the security of your own device and browser environment, and for maintaining any confidentiality or privilege obligations that apply to the files you screen.

How We Use Information

  • To create and maintain your account and authenticate your login
  • To process subscription billing, renewals, and payment status
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and reliability of CourtReady
  • To communicate with you about your account, billing, or material changes to the service

We do not use your account information for advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles based on your use of CourtReady.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

APOM Research does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, account data, or any other information collected through CourtReady to any third party for money or other consideration.

Data Sharing

We do not share your account information or any other data collected through CourtReady with third parties, including law enforcement, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate CourtReady (such as our payment processor and hosting infrastructure), under confidentiality and data-protection obligations, and only to the extent necessary for them to perform their function
  • When required to comply with a valid, legally binding order, subpoena, or court order that we determine, after review, to be enforceable against us
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of APOM Research, our users, or others, where legally permitted

Because file content you screen is generally never transmitted to or stored on our servers in the first place, in the ordinary course there is nothing of that nature for us to disclose in response to a third-party request.

Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect account and billing information, including encryption in transit and access controls limiting who may reach our infrastructure. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Because file screening happens locally on your device, the security of the files themselves also depends on the security of your own device, browser, and network.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain account and billing information for as long as your account is active, and for a limited period afterward as needed to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations, or to resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your account and associated account information at any time by emailing hello@apomarchives.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including the right to know what account information we hold, request a copy of it, request correction, or request deletion. To exercise these rights, email hello@apomarchives.com with the subject line "CourtReady Privacy Request."

Intended Users

CourtReady is intended for use by licensed attorneys and their authorized staff in a professional capacity. It is not directed at, or intended for use by, the general public or minors, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will change. If we make material changes to how we handle your information, we will make reasonable efforts to notify account holders.
