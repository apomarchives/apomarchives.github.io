Why It Matters

Why This Matters For Your Case

Digital evidence is increasingly central to family law — and increasingly challenged.

Common vulnerabilities I eliminate:

  • Screenshots without metadata (easily challenged as fabricated)
  • Timestamps without verification (capture time vs. post time)
  • Evidence without chain of custody (gaps are ammunition for opposing counsel)
  • Scattered documentation that overwhelms judges and loses impact
  • Opposing evidence that goes unchallenged because no one reviewed it technically

My documentation protocols produce evidence packages that are organized, authenticated, and ready for direct use.

Standards

Admissibility Standards I Build To

Every evidence package is constructed to meet the authentication requirements courts actually apply:

  • Documented collection methodology with hash verification FRE 901(b)(9)
  • Machine-generated records with hash certification FRE 902(13-14)
  • Documented handling from collection through court submission with no evidentiary gaps Chain of Custody
  • Timestamp verification distinguishing capture time from post time, with platform-native confirmation Metadata Integrity
  • Packages structured to federal referral requirements for interstate harassment and cyberstalking cases FBI Standards

Work product can be structured to support attorney-client privilege where applicable. Expert witness testimony available on request.
