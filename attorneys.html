Why This Matters For Your Case

Digital evidence is increasingly central to family law cases — but it's also increasingly challenged.

Common Vulnerabilities I Eliminate:

  • Screenshots without metadata (easily challenged as fabricated)
  • Timestamps without verification (prove capture time, not post time)
  • Evidence without chain of custody (gaps = ammunition for opposing counsel)
  • Scattered documentation (overwhelming judges, losing impact)

My documentation protocols produce evidence packages that are organized, authenticated, and ready for court — or formatted for FBI referral when federal crimes are involved.
