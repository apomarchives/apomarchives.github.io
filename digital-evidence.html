Why Screenshots Aren't Enough

You have hundreds of screenshots. You've documented everything. So why isn't anyone taking you seriously?

Because screenshots alone prove nothing.

  • They don't prove when content was posted — only when you captured it
  • They don't preserve metadata that authenticates the evidence
  • They don't establish chain of custody
  • They can be easily challenged as fabricated or altered
  • They don't connect the dots between accounts, patterns, and coordinated behavior

Courts and law enforcement need more. They need evidence documented to the same standards used in federal investigations.
