Legal — StayGone

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026 | Last Updated: July 9, 2026

Short version: StayGone does not sell your data. We do not share your incident logs, audit answers, or other content with any third party, including law enforcement, except when we are legally required to do so. Evidence entries you create are hashed with SHA-256 to help demonstrate they haven't been altered. This page explains exactly what we collect, why, and who (if anyone) ever sees it.

Who We Are

StayGone is a safety documentation and digital-security application published by APOM Research, designed to help survivors of domestic abuse and coercive control log incidents, evaluate device and account security, and prepare organized records for personal safety planning, legal proceedings, or law enforcement use.

Contact: hello@apomarchives.com

Information We Collect

We collect only the categories of information described below. We do not collect information from your device or accounts automatically beyond what is necessary to operate the app and process payment.

  • Incident logs — any text, dates, descriptions, or other details you manually enter into the app to document an incident.
  • Device and account audit answers — your responses to in-app questions designed to help you assess whether your devices or accounts show signs of unauthorized access or monitoring.
  • Jurisdiction — the state, province, or country you tell us you are located in, used to show you relevant legal resources and safety information.
  • Subscription status — your plan, entitlement, and billing status, processed on our behalf by our subscription management provider, RevenueCat.

We do not require you to use your real name, and we encourage you to use whatever identifying information you feel is safe to provide.

How We Use Your Information

  • To let you create, store, view, and organize your own incident logs and audit answers within the app.
  • To generate a SHA-256 hash of each evidence entry at the time it is created, so that you and anyone you choose to share it with can later verify the entry has not been altered.
  • To show you jurisdiction-relevant safety and legal resource information.
  • To manage your subscription, billing, and entitlements through RevenueCat.
  • To respond to you if you contact us for support.

We do not use your incident logs or audit answers for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the app's core functionality to you.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

APOM Research does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, incident logs, or audit answers to any third party for money or any other form of consideration. This has always been true and will remain true.

How Evidence Hashing Works

When you create an incident log or evidence entry, StayGone generates a SHA-256 cryptographic hash of that entry's content. A hash is a fixed-length digital fingerprint: if the underlying entry is changed in any way, even slightly, the hash will no longer match. This allows you, an attorney, or a court to verify that an entry has not been altered since it was created.

Hashing verifies integrity — it does not encrypt or hide the content of your entry, and it does not by itself guarantee that any court or agency will accept an entry as evidence. See the Disclaimers section of our Terms of Use for more on this.

Data Sharing — Including Law Enforcement

We do not share your data with third parties, including law enforcement, except as required by law. Specifically:

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your incident logs, audit answers, jurisdiction information, or any other content you enter into StayGone to any third party — including law enforcement agencies, government bodies, employers, family members, or partners — for any reason other than a legally valid, binding order that compels us to do so, such as a properly issued subpoena, warrant, or court order.

We do not grant any government agency or private party standing "backdoor" or ongoing access to user data. We do not proactively monitor, scan, or report the contents of incident logs or audit answers to law enforcement.

Where we receive a legal demand that we determine, after review, to be valid and enforceable, we will comply only to the extent legally required. Unless we are legally prohibited from doing so, or believe notice would endanger someone's safety, we will attempt to notify the affected user before any disclosure so they have the opportunity to seek to narrow or challenge the request through their own counsel.

We will never disclose your information to an abusive partner, family member, or any other individual based solely on a claimed relationship, without a valid, binding legal order directed at us specifically.

If you choose to export your own data — for example, to send a hashed evidence report to your attorney or to law enforcement yourself — that sharing is initiated and controlled entirely by you, not by us.

Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on a small number of service providers to operate StayGone. These providers only receive the minimum information necessary to perform their function, are contractually restricted from using your data for their own purposes, and are prohibited from selling it.

  • RevenueCat — manages subscription billing, renewals, and entitlement status. RevenueCat receives your subscription and purchase information but does not receive your incident logs or audit answers. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • App store platforms (Apple App Store / Google Play) — process your initial payment per their own respective privacy policies; we receive only limited purchase and entitlement data back from these platforms via RevenueCat.
  • Infrastructure and hosting providers — provide the servers and storage that allow the app to function, under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

Third-Party Recommendations & Affiliate Disclosure

StayGone may recommend third-party privacy and security tools that can help strengthen your digital safety, such as Proton (encrypted email and related services) and Signal (encrypted messaging). Some of these recommendations include affiliate links, meaning APOM Research may earn a commission if you choose to sign up through our link, at no additional cost to you.

These affiliate relationships do not influence which tools we recommend, and using a recommended link is always optional — you are free to use any tool you prefer, or to reach these services directly without our link. We do not receive any information about your usage of these third-party tools once you leave StayGone, and your relationship with those providers is governed entirely by their own terms and privacy policies.

Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit, access controls limiting who can reach app infrastructure, and SHA-256 hashing of evidence entries to support tamper-evidence. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and while we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you are concerned your device itself may be compromised or monitored (for example, by someone with physical or remote access to your phone), please review the in-app device and account audit, and consider using a device the person you are documenting does not have access to.

Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete individual incident log entries, or your entire account and all associated data, at any time from within the app. When you delete your account, we delete your incident logs, audit answers, and jurisdiction information from our active systems within 30 days, except where a copy must be retained for a limited period to comply with a legal obligation, resolve a dispute, or enforce our agreements.

You can also request deletion by emailing hello@apomarchives.com from the email associated with your account.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable privacy laws, including the right to:

  • Know what information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability)
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of the sale of data (we do not sell data, so there is nothing to opt out of)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@apomarchives.com with the subject line "Privacy Request."

Children's Privacy

StayGone is intended for adults 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

International Users

Your jurisdiction information is used only to tailor the safety and legal resources shown to you inside the app. It is not shared with any government body, and it is handled under the same protections described in the "Data Sharing" section above regardless of where you are located.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will change. If we make material changes to how we handle your information, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users within the app.
