Why This Matters

Screenshots alone aren't enough.

They don't prove when something was posted — only when you captured it. They don't preserve metadata. They don't establish chain of custody. And without proper documentation, even the most damning evidence can be dismissed.

I use the same federal-grade protocols used in FBI investigations to ensure your evidence holds up.
