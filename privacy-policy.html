PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 19, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 19, 2026

Short version: APOM Research does not sell your data, does not use advertising trackers, and does not place cookies on your device. Client information shared during consultations is held in strict confidence.

WHO WE ARE

APOM Research is a professional OSINT investigation and digital forensics practice operated by Kyērstin O'Neal. We provide cyberstalking investigation, digital evidence development, and intelligence services to individuals, attorneys, and advocates.

Contact: hello@apomarchives.com

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information in two ways: information you provide directly, and limited technical data collected automatically.

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, and case description when you contact us via email
  • Scheduling information when you book a consultation through Calendly
  • Payment information when you purchase products through Kit (handled by Kit's platform)
  • Referral information submitted via email

Information collected automatically:

  • Page views and basic traffic data via Plausible Analytics (see below)

We do not run contact forms that collect data on this website. All direct contact is via email or the Calendly scheduling tool.

ANALYTICS: PLAUSIBLE

This website uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics tool that is fully GDPR compliant. Plausible does not use cookies, does not track individual users across sessions, does not collect personal data, and does not share data with advertising networks.

The data Plausible collects is limited to: page URL, referrer source, browser type, operating system, device type, and country. No personally identifiable information is ever collected or stored. Your answers on the Safety Audit tool (safety.apomarchives.com) never leave your device — they are processed entirely in your browser.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Calendly — used for scheduling consultations. When you book a call, Calendly collects your name, email, and scheduling preferences under Calendly's Privacy Policy.
  • Kit (formerly ConvertKit) — used to deliver digital products (Evidence Toolkit, Subpoena Templates). Purchases are subject to Kit's Privacy Policy.
  • LinkedIn — linked from the footer. Visiting LinkedIn is subject to LinkedIn's own privacy practices.
  • GitHub Pages — this website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server log data per their privacy policy.

COOKIES

This website does not place cookies on your device. Plausible Analytics operates without cookies. If you visit Calendly or Kit through links on this site, those services may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

CLIENT CONFIDENTIALITY

Information shared during consultations or in the course of an engagement is held in strict professional confidence. APOM Research does not disclose client identities, case details, or investigation content to any third party without explicit written consent, except as required by law.

Work product may be structured to support attorney-client privilege where applicable. Please discuss privilege arrangements with your attorney.

DATA RETENTION

Email correspondence is retained for business record purposes. Scheduling data is managed by Calendly per their retention policies. Analytics data in Plausible is retained per our Plausible account settings and does not contain personally identifiable information.

If you wish to have email correspondence deleted, contact us at hello@apomarchives.com and we will accommodate reasonable requests where not required for legal or business purposes.

YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable privacy laws, including:

  • The right to know what information we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to opt out of data sale (we do not sell data)
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@apomarchives.com with the subject line "Privacy Request."

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This website and its services are intended for adults 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, contact us immediately so we can delete it.

SECURITY

We take reasonable precautions to protect information transmitted to us. All email communication is conducted over standard encrypted email protocols. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information sent via email.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will change. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without clear notice.
